About

Here at Shook Hills All Natural Beef, I have taken these high producing, muscled and marbled cows and crossed them with large framed, high marbling registered Angus bulls. The final product is a calf raised by a mama with rich, hardy butterfat milk, great muscling and a herd sire bull with a background of producing grade A beef, large tender steaks, with tremendous marbling and tenderness. A final product of home raised beef that is second to none. I have a limited supply only of these outstanding beef from year to year. I am very choosy about the steers chosen to feed out. Please feel free to call and talk to me personally about any questions or comments that you may have or to place an order.

Thank you for your business! Sincerely... owner Matt Strunk Learn More

Setting our beef apart

We sell only beef we raise here at Shook Hills. No growth implants or growth stimulants. No hormones. No subtheraputic or topical antibiotics. Our cattle graze on unlimited open pastures, and are supplemented with a vitamin, mineral, and corn diet. Click here for more information!
