Setting our beef apart

We sell only beef we raise here at Rising S Ranch. No growth implants or growth stimulants. No hormones. No subtheraputic or topical antibiotics. Our cattle graze on unlimited open pastures, and are supplemented with a vitamin, mineral, and non-GMO corn diet (when available).

My personal promise to you is to deliver a clean, healthy, hormone/antibiotic free beef to you and your family. Here at Rising S Ranch, we believe in quality not quantity. I have been raising beef for over 25 years. This is a product I am proud of and pleased to be able to share with you and your family.

Why Tarantaise/Angus?

About

Here at Rising S Ranch, I have taken these high producing, muscled and marbled Tarantaise cows and crossed them with large framed, high marbling registered Angus bulls. The final product is a calf raised by a mama with rich, hardy butterfat milk, great muscling and a herd sire bull with a background of producing grade A beef, large tender steaks, with tremendous marbling and tenderness. A final product of home raised beef that is second to none. I have a limited supply only of these outstanding beef from year to year. I am very choosy about the steers chosen to feed out. Please feel free to call and talk to me personally about any questions or comments that you may have or to place an order.
